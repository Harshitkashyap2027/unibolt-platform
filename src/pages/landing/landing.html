Platform Features

Everything you need to succeed

A complete ecosystem designed to help students find the perfect internship and help employers discover exceptional talent.

AI Smart Matching

Our Gemini-powered AI analyzes your resume and matches you to internships with a precise percentage score, highlighting your strengths and gaps.

  • Resume keyword extraction
  • Semantic skill matching
  • Match score with explanations

Interactive Skill Trees

Visualize your learning path as a directed acyclic graph. Unlock skills sequentially — from Python to PyTorch to Computer Vision.

  • SVG-based DAG visualization
  • Prerequisite dependency validation
  • 4 skill categories

ELO Ranking System

Compete on coding challenges and peer reviews. Your ELO rating evolves dynamically using the same math behind chess rankings.

  • Dynamic K-factor system
  • Win/Loss/Draw mechanics
  • Real-time leaderboard

Real-Time Chat

Instant messaging between students and employers. Firestore-powered with live message delivery, read receipts, and online status.

  • Real-time message delivery
  • Online/offline indicators
  • Conversation history

Achievement System

Earn badges and achievements for completing skill trees, landing interviews, and competing on the leaderboard. Gamify your job search.

  • Unlockable badges
  • Progress tracking
  • Public profile showcase

Automated Screening

Cloud Functions automatically extract keywords from applications, rank candidates, and notify employers — all within seconds of submission.

  • Instant keyword extraction
  • Auto-ranking applicants
  • Employer notifications
